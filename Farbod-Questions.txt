
Correlation

Research Question: Is there a correlation between daily cloud cover and sunshine duration in London?

Dependent Variable: sunshine (Interval)
Independent Variable: cloud_cover (Interval)

Null hypothesis (H0): There is no correlation between daily cloud cover and sunshine duration in London.
Alternative hypothesis (H1): There is a correlation between daily cloud cover and sunshine duration in London.

Comparison of Mean

Research Question: Is there a difference in the mean daily global radiation between Summer and Winter in London?

Dependent Variable: global_radiation (Interval)

Independent Variable: date → Season 

Null hypothesis (H0): There is no difference in the mean daily global radiation between Summer and Winter.
Alternative hypothesis (H1): There is a difference in the mean daily global radiation between Summer and Winter.

Comparison of Proportions

Research Question: Is the proportion of high-pressure days (pressure > 102000 Pa) different between Autumn and Spring in London?

Dependent Variable: pressure → HighPressureDay (Nominal: Yes if > 102000, No if ≤ 102000)
Independent Variable: date → Season (Nominal: Autumn vs. Spring)

Null hypothesis (H0): There is no difference in the proportion of high-pressure days between Autumn and Spring.
Alternative hypothesis (H1): There is a difference in the proportion of high-pressure days between Autumn and Spring.
