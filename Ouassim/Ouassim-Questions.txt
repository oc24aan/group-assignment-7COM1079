Project Title:
How windy is each season? a seasonal comparison of daily wind speed in London (1979–2020)

Research Question:
Does the average daily wind speed differ across the four seasons in London?

Dependent Variable:
	•	wind_speed (Interval)

Independent Variable:
	•	date → Season (Nominal)

Null hypothesis (H0):
There is no difference in the mean daily wind speed among the seasons in London.

Alternative hypothesis (H1):
There is a difference in the mean daily wind speed among the seasons.

Comparison of porportions

Project Title:
Are sunny days more common in summer? a seasonal look at sunshine hours in London

Research Question:
Is the proportion of days with more than 2 hours of sunshine higher in Summer compared to Winter?

Dependent Variable:
	•	sunshine → SunnyDay (Nominal: yes / no (boolean))

Independent Variable:
	•	date → Season (Nominal)

Null hypothesis (H0):
There is no difference in the proportion of sunny days between Summer and Winter.

Alternative hypothesis (H1):
There is a difference in the proportion of sunny days between the two seasons.
